┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🎤 SAMPLE CONSULTATION (Read this when recording)                           │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Doctor: Good morning! What brings you in today?
  
  Patient: I've been having severe headaches for the past week. 
  They start in the morning and get worse throughout the day.
  
  Doctor: Can you describe the pain?
  
  Patient: It's throbbing pain on the right side. Sometimes I feel nauseous.
  
  Doctor: Have you had headaches like this before?
  
  Patient: Yes, about 6 months ago, but they went away.
  
  Doctor: Are you taking any medications?
  
  Patient: Just my blood pressure medication.
  
  Doctor: Let me check your blood pressure.
  Your blood pressure is 145 over 90, slightly elevated. 
  Pupils are reactive. No neurological deficits.
  
  Doctor: This appears to be migraine headaches.
  I'm prescribing sumatriptan 50mg for when you feel a headache.
  Keep a headache diary. Avoid caffeine and alcohol.
  Drink plenty of water. Come back in two weeks.
  
  Patient: Thank you, doctor.

│ 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING                                                           │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  "Failed to access microphone"
    → Grant microphone permissions in browser

  "Could not understand audio"
    → Speak clearly, check microphone is working

  "Audio conversion failed"
    → Install FFmpeg from https://ffmpeg.org

  "Basic transcription without AI"
    → Set GOOGLE_API_KEY environment variable
